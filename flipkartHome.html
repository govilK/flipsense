Online shopping India

Forget crowded malls, endless maze-like parking lots and long lines. From clothes to mobiles phones, you can buy just about anything online. And when we say say online, we mean Flipkart.com, one of India's best online shopping destinations.

Shopping on Flipkart.com
What is it like to shop on Flipkart you ask? Well, let's make it simple for you. Say you have to buy a baby shower present for your expecting friend, all you need to do in log into Flipkart.com and browse through the countless gifting options available for you. And that's not all, you can have the product of your choice gift wrapped with your own personalized message and have it delivered right to your friend's doorstep. Awesome, right? No more trips to the mall. No more getting stuck in endless traffic. With Flipkart, you can replace *honk* *honk* with *click* *click*. It's that easy.

Ab Har Wish Hogi Poori with Flipkart
At Flipkart, we believe that a wish is a desire that should be fulfilled for every Indian. And that is why you'll see that there's always something for you to shop for. Whether it's an IIT-JEE entrance book or the latest Mi phone, you'll find it here on Flipkart. Home to a huge collection of home furnishing products, appliances, footwear, television, clothes, mobiles, books, cameras and laptops, the list is as endless as the potential long line that you would end up in should you happen to brave the crowd at the end of your shopping trip at a mall.

Enjoy delightful prices
For 'Every Wish Fulfilled' to happen, we at Flipkart understand that prices can put you off. And that is why we have round-the-clock discounts and amazing prices for you to enjoy. With options like Deals-of-the-Day, find products such as apparel, toys, refrigerators, air-conditioners and healthy and beauty products at unbelievably low prices.

Happy and safe shopping every time
When you log into Flipkart.com, you will find that all the fun elements of shopping in malls have been exceptionally replicated. The easy-to-use interface that includes filters, compare options, buying guides, interactive descriptions and reviews, you'll see that shopping on Flipkart is fun and easy.

Dedicated to fulfilling your wishes without any hassle, Flipkart ensures that everything from placing an order to having it delivered right to doorstep goes smoothly and efficiently. We at Flipkart understand that your personal information such as bank details are of utmost importance and because of that all your online payment, whether it is through credit/debit card or net banking transactions, are safe and secure. Still apprehensive about paying online, we got you covered with our ever-popular cash-on-delivery option. And with fast delivery options, you'll have your product in your hand in no time. Apart from all that, should you be unhappy with your order, we have got excellent exchange and replacement policies that will surely change your frown into a big smile.

Shop on the App
Oh yes, we're available on the small screen too. Just go to Google Play Store or Apple App Store, download the Flipkart app onto your smartphone or tablet, and enjoy shopping on the go. The app has an interactive and easy user interface and you'll be able to enjoy app-only deals, get notifications about offers and order status, and plenty more. Download the app and you'll know what we are talking about.
